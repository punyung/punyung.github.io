About Me

Posted on 2023-04-16

Welcome to Rong’s place! This is a brief introduction about myself.

I received B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Guangdong University of Technology, after which I started mphil training at Chinese Academy of Science, working in National Genomics Data Center (NGDC), while advised by Professor Zhang. Now I am working on my mphil thesis which is centered around batch effect adjustment of high-throughput sequencing data. Besides, I actively engaged in database research projects (ICG, GEN) at NGDC, helping to curate biological data in our country.[1]-[3]
